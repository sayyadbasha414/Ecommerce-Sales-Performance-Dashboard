🛒 E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of e-commerce sales performance across products, regions, customer segments, and shipping methods. The solution helps business stakeholders monitor revenue growth, profitability, customer trends, and operational performance through interactive visualizations and KPIs.

🎯 Business Objectives
Track Year-to-Date (YTD) sales performance.
Monitor profit margins and sales growth.
Identify top and bottom-performing products.
Analyze regional sales distribution.
Evaluate shipping mode performance.
Support strategic business decisions through data-driven insights.
